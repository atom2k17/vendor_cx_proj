About Simworks

At Simworks, we are creating a comprehensive marketplace for engineering simulation services. Our platform connects consumers, from startups to big industrial organizations, with highly skilled engineering solution providers (ESPs).

Our mission is improving access to simulation tools and services, lowering prices, and accelerating innovation. Whether you're a product company seeking for the proper vendor or a service provider looking for new prospects, Simworks bridges the gap by providing a transparent marketplace platform based on real project histories and user feedback.
